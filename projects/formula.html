Role Battery Pack Lead
Timeline 2023 — Present
Stack SolidWorks, HV Systems

Engineering for Performance.

Leading the accumulator subteam at Olin Electric Motorsports involves balancing extreme power requirements with strict safety regulations. Our goal was to create a modular battery pack that powers the MK.V vehicle.

Battery Architecture.

The system utilizes a custom BMS (Battery Management System) integrated with a 600V tractive system. My work focused on the mechanical housing, thermal management, and ensuring isolation between high-voltage and low-voltage systems.
