Olin Electric Motorsports

Olin Electric Motorsport is a student-run FSAE team based in Needham, MA. As the Accumulator Subteam Lead, I managed and mentored a team of 10 engineers, ensuring HV safety, and oversee the project timeline. Additionally, I am designing and fabricating the 400V battery and working closely with our Electrical subteam for subsystem integration.

01 / Battery Design

Engineering for High Voltage

The design process began with extensive CAD modeling in SolidWorks to determine the most efficient layout for the lithium-ion cells. I performed Finite Element Analysis (FEA) on the container structure to ensure it could withstand the required G-loads during competition.

02 / Segment Manufacturing

Precision Fabrication

Fabrication involved high-precision assembly of busbars and maintenance-plugs. Every component was machined to tight tolerances to ensure electrical connectivity and structural integrity under racing conditions.

03 / Segment Assembly

Module Integration

Assembly required strict adherence to safety protocols to prevent arcing. Each segment was isolated and tested before being integrated into the main accumulator housing.

04 / Testing

Validation & Safety

Before hitting the track, the accumulator undergoes rigorous testing, including isolation monitoring checks and full-load discharge cycles to validate thermal models.
